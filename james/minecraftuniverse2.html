What is MineCraft?

Minecraft is a game made of blocks and is usuelly described as a digital version of lego, there is no story line and you can literally do what erver you want to do, the possibilities are endless. There are a bunch of different versions like pocket edision on a tablet or Ipad, xbox 360 edition, Minecraft PC and a bunch more on different consoles. There are 3 modes on PC but most versions have only two modes, Creative and Survival. On Creative you have unlimited resourses to build with and is mainly for building. Survival is where you have to collect food, kill monsters that come out in caves and at night and you do not have unlimited resourses, you have to collect and craft them your self.
